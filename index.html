How to Play

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in six attempts. Each time you guess, the tiles will change color to indicate how close your guess is to the target word:

  • Correct: The letter is in the correct position.
  • Present: The letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
  • Absent: The letter is not in the word at all.

The category for this game is: Types of Fruits.

Use the input box to type your guess and hit "Submit" to check it. Good luck!
